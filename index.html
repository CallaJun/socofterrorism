Boko Haram

  1. "People Committed to the Propagation of the Prophet's Teachings and Jihad"
  2. Emerged ≈2003 under Mohammed Yusuf
  3. Before 2009: Recruited thousands of followers, many from poor families and between 17-30, fought for establishment of Sharia law in Borno State
  4. 2009 - Boko Haram "uprising" - Yusuf is killed, and second-in-command Abubakar Shekau rises to power
  5. 2009 - present: Tactics more aligned with global terrorist organizations - focused on all of Nigeria, significantly more violent

Overview

There are pre-existing problems in Nigeria that stem from a corrupt government - including a failing economy and its police force's use of excessive force/extrajudicial killings.

Boko Haram provides extreme examples of these issues, and ones that illustrate how corruption/high unemployment rates/excessive force harms the government.
